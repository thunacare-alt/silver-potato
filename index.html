Deep Dive

Production Agent — In Detail

End-to-end tracking across both production lines with 44 data points, shift-level QC, and a Google Sheets dashboard.

♻️

Plastic AFR Line

20 DATA POINTS PER SHIFT

From MSW and supplier-sourced plastic waste through cleaning, shredding and baling into compacted alternate fuel for cement kilns.

Trommel Manual Segregation Magnet Primary Shredder Secondary Shredder Baler 100T → Compacted AFR Bales

Start-of-Shift

Staff count (skilled/unskilled) Opening meter reading Input feedstock (by source) Opening stock

End-of-Shift

Closing meter reading Bale count & avg weight Downtime & reason Dispatch by customer

Quality Control

Conditional — "Sample taken?" → Moisture, ash, calorific value
🌾

Agri Pellet Line

24 DATA POINTS PER SHIFT

Paddy straw and mustard residues processed into uniform biomass pellets for thermal power plants and FMCG boilers.

Shredder (10-15mm) Hammer Mill (2-8mm) Cyclone Pelletizer → Biomass Pellets

Start-of-Shift

Staff & operator count Opening meter & hour meter Agri-residue received (t) Opening stock

End-of-Shift

Closing meter & hour meter Pellet output (t) Downtime & reason Dispatch & freight rate

Supplier Tracking

Geography, tonnage, freight rates per supplier — tracked per dispatch

Security

Built for enterprise trust

Zero-trust access control with role-based boundaries — no agent can share what it shouldn't.

🔒 Role-Based Access

Three tiers — Super Admin, Admin, User. Every interaction is checked against the sender's role before any data is shared.

🛡️ Data Protection

Employee PII, pricing, infrastructure details — never shared outside authorized roles. Hard-coded rules that cannot be overridden.

📋 Full Audit Trail

Every message, every tool call, every decision is logged with timestamps. Complete traceability for compliance and governance.
